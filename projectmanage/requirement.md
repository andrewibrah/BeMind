
GOAL: Create a Mobile Reminders app that is light weight, coach-like and relieable

Core features:

Group Reminders : 
- Create task bundles depending on your life style.
- default : daily to dos, custom Next day planning, Multi-day tasks (Goal similarity)

Custom/Check in Reminders: 
- not just a reminder, but taking in if you actually began, completed or skiped your task. 
- Swipe down on your messages and log with a defualt structure 
- Customize How you track : Timed check ins, Optional inputs, log progress or check in statement. 

Progress and Accountablity: 
- Track your consisently, effort and progress. 
- Encouraging yourself to keep going.
- Ensure steps are set and completed for the future you strive for. 

Historic Data:
- Task history success sorted daily
- time due - check in - checked in metrics 
- Daily vs Todays vs future task insights
